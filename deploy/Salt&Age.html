salt&age

A project I was given was to create a fictional publication. My publication, "salt&age", was about cheese from around the world and its accompaniments when served. It's fascinating how cheese is made because there is so much that goes into it that most people wouldn't expect.

I was influenced by New York Times Magazine. The focus of the project was to create a cover, table of contents, and a main story layout including the opening spread, as well as the following spreads.
