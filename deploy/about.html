Who I am

I enjoy working on UX/UI design, branding, and typography, with a focus on accessibility and research. I would like to help people through design. I love assisting everyone through empathic design and accessibility. Making sure that design is accessible to all ensures that it’s useable for anyone regardless of circumstance. I belive that good design shouldn’t be obvious. It should be a seamless form of communication between the end product and its users.

In my free time, I play electric bass, geek out over public transit, and I love to cook. I also love going out on adventures with my camera to a new part of whatever area I am in.
